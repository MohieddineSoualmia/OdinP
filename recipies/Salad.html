Greek Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • 200g feta cheese, cubed
  • 100g kalamata olives
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Combine cucumber, tomatoes, onion, and olives in a bowl.
  2. Top with feta cheese.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss gently before serving.
