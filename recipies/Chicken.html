Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 150g yogurt
  • 2 tbsp tikka masala spice mix
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g tomato sauce
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for 1 hour.
  2. Grill or bake chicken until cooked.
  3. Saut√© onion and garlic, add tomato sauce, then simmer.
  4. Add chicken and cream, cook for 10 mins.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice.
