Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 200g flour
  • 100g butter, softened
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 50g white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 150g chocolate chips
  • ½ tsp baking soda

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream butter and sugars, then mix in egg and vanilla.
  3. Add flour and baking soda, then fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop dough onto a baking tray and bake for 10-12 mins.
  5. Let cool before serving.
