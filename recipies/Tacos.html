Beef Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 100g shredded cheese
  • Sour cream & salsa (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Brown beef in a pan, then add taco seasoning and water.
  2. Simmer for 5 mins.
  3. Warm taco shells as per package instructions.
  4. Fill shells with beef, lettuce, tomato, and cheese.
  5. Top with sour cream and salsa if desired.
